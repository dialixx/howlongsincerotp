If you're here and you somehow didn't learn about Monolith along the way, it's a top down shmup roguelike which bears an incredible amount of polish in its current state with its DLC. I highly recommend you check it and its DLC out, for like 15 bucks total it's an incredible experience. Here's a couple links for you.

The steam page for Monolith: https://store.steampowered.com/app/603960/Monolith/
(Many veterans of the game suggest you buy both the base-game and DLC together for a smoother experience, as the DLC provides many quality of life features as well as a whole sea of new content that meets or surpasses the quality of the base-game content.)

The official discord for the community, where you can talk to the devs and other people who play Monolith:
https://discord.gg/46BqQ2K

The community wiki for the game, which while still under construction, contains much of the information you'll need to know to play the game (some information may need to be corrected, and spoilers of course!):
https://monolith.miraheze.org/wiki/Monolith:_After_The_End_Wiki

If you ever decide to speedrun the game, check this out and try to beat Nick's records while you're at it:
https://www.speedrun.com/monolith

Or maybe these links are places you already frequent, and you came to count the days. So be it.
